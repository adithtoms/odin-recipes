Description

This gingersnap cookie recipe makes gingersnap cookies that melt in your mouth. I received this ginger cookie recipe from my grandmother;

Ingredeints

  • flour
  • spices
  • molasses

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together multiple times to ensure no lumps are left
  2. Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture until dough forms
  3. Bake in a preheated oven until the tops are rounded and slightly cracked.
